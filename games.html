02 Goldbach challenge

Every even number greater than 2 is the sum of two primes. Goldbach conjectured this in 1742. It has never been disproven - and never been proven. Enter an even number and explore all its decompositions.

Enter an even number to see all its Goldbach decompositions.

Goldbach comet (pairs count per even n)

Each point shows how many ways an even number can be expressed as p + q. The pattern never reaches zero - that would disprove Goldbach's conjecture.

04 Are you prime?

Enter something about yourself - your name, your birth year, your full date of birth. The site converts it to a number and tells you everything about it. Are you prime?
